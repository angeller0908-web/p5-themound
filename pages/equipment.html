EQUIPMENT DATABASE

A catalog of Spanish military weapons, navigational equipment, medical supplies, and ancient bioluminescent devices. Prepare your loadout wisely before descending.

Item Name Category Weight (kg) Sanity Protection Tactical Description
Arquebus of Coronado Weapon 8.5 kg 0% Heavy 16th-century matchlock firearm. Deals massive kinetic damage. Easily stuns Gug giants but requires a 6-second stationary reload.
Toledo Steel Halberd Weapon 4.2 kg 5% Polearm offering excellent melee range. Allows sweeping slashes that can hit multiple blind Y'm-bhi dead at once.
Officer Toledo Sword Weapon 1.8 kg 5% Lightweight slashing weapon. Consumes minimal stamina, allowing rapid strikes. Perfect for nimble scout builds.
Matchlock Pistol Weapon 2.5 kg 0% Compact secondary firearm. Deals high damage at short range. Quick to draw when ambushed, but generates heavy noise.
Crossbow of Zamacona Weapon 3.5 kg 0% A modified steel crossbow. Fires heavy bolts. Completely silent operation—allows sniping sentries without alerting nearby packs.
Obsidian Macuahuitl Weapon 2.2 kg 0% Aztec-style club lined with volcanic obsidian blades. Deals high bleeding damage but has low durability against stone armor.
Lantern of K'n-yan Light Source 2.0 kg 30% Subterranean lamp fueled by glowing red lichen extract. Decreases sanity decay rate of all squad members within 6 meters.
Spanish Iron Torches Light Source 0.8 kg 10% Standard pitch-soaked torch. Cheap and light. Can be thrown onto ground to illuminate dark corridors permanently. Burns for 5 minutes.
Photic Focus Lamp Light Source 3.0 kg 15% Heavy spotlight that fires a condensed blue biolume beam. Direct hits will temporarily blind and freeze Formless Spawn.
Cthulhian Astral Lens Light Source 4.0 kg 50% An ancient, heavy lens that projects purple void light. Reveals hidden pathways, invisible murals, and subterranean gravity traps.
Magnetic Brass Compass Navigation 0.2 kg 0% Navigational compass. In K'n-yan, it points toward active extraction gateway mounds instead of magnetic north.
Map of Zamacona Navigation 0.1 kg 0% Historical parchment outlining level boundaries. Reveals the location of ancient safe-zones and botanical lichen chambers.
Sound Decoy Bells Exploration Tool 0.5 kg 0% Throw-able bells. Emits ringing noises upon landing, drawing the blind Y'm-bhi away from crucial doors or chest locations.
Spanish Steel Trap Exploration Tool 3.2 kg 0% Heavy spring trap. Stops charging Gugs and Stalkers in their tracks, dealing moderate damage and locking them for 8 seconds.
Biolume Sedative Vials Medical 0.2 kg 0% Crushed sedative flowers in spring water. Restores 25 Sanity points instantly, clearing minor audio hallucinations.
Cinnabar Elixir Medical 0.3 kg 0% A rich, red alchemical oil. Restores 40 Health points over a 10-second duration. Breaks if the player takes damage during use.
Laudanum Dropper Medical 0.2 kg 80% Highly potent narcotic. Completely stops sanity decay for 120 seconds. Side-effect: blurs the edges of screen.
Aromatic Smelling Salts Medical 0.1 kg 0% pungent crystal tube. Can be crushed near an unconscious or fully insane teammate to revive them to active duty instantly.

RECOMMENDED SQUAD LOADOUTS

Synergize your gear setup based on your group size to maximize extraction ratios.

Duo Scavengers (2 Players)

Perfect for quick, quiet surface and red-vault relic runs. Emphasizes stealth and speed.

PLAYER 1 (Point Scout):

  • Officer Toledo Sword
  • Crossbow of Zamacona
  • Magnetic Brass Compass
  • Biolume Sedatives

PLAYER 2 (Support Light):

  • Lantern of K'n-yan
  • Toledo Steel Halberd
  • Sound Decoy Bells (x3)
  • Cinnabar Elixir (x2)

Ruin Infiltrators (3 Players)

Balanced setup designed to breach level 2 Yoth basalt chambers safely.

PLAYER 1 (Breacher):

  • Arquebus of Coronado
  • Matchlock Pistol
  • Spanish Steel Trap

PLAYER 2 (Lantern Guard):

  • Lantern of K'n-yan
  • Toledo Steel Halberd
  • Laudanum Dropper (x2)

PLAYER 3 (Navigator):

  • Photic Focus Lamp
  • Officer Toledo Sword
  • Map & Compass Combo

Expedition Vanguard (4 Players)

Maximum safety for entering N'kai. Heavy defensive firepower and utility.

PLAYER 1: Heavy Fire Support

Arquebus + Toledo Sword + Steel Trap

PLAYER 2: Sanity Guardian

Cthulhian Astral Lens + Laudanum + Salts

PLAYER 3: Tactical Scout

Crossbow + Decoy Bells + Compass

PLAYER 4: Frontline Vanguard

Steel Halberd + Focus Lamp + Cinnabar Elixir
