With the highly anticipated launch of The Mound: Omen of Cthulhu scheduled for July 15, 2026, PC players are looking for the best storefronts to secure their copy. Beyond purchasing directly on Steam, authorized third-party retailers like Fanatical have officially listed the game for pre-order.

Using authorized retailers offers an alternative way to purchase Steam keys, especially for players looking to stack store reward points or utilize existing digital wallets.

Direct Steam Purchase vs. Authorized Key Sellers

When preparing for this cooperative PVE extraction shooter, it is essential to source your game keys from licensed distributors. Fanatical is a fully authorized partner of publisher Nacon, meaning keys purchased there support the developers at ACE Team directly and are guaranteed to activate without issue on Steam.

Buying from approved key sellers often comes with minor perks, such as store-specific loyalty rewards, coupons for future purchases, or promotional bundles. However, unlike Steam's direct store page, refund policies on third-party keys can be more restrictive once a key has been revealed or activated. If you value Steam's two-hour play window refund policy, buying directly through Steam remains the safest path.

Choosing Between Standard and Deluxe Editions

The upcoming title features two main packages on both Steam and Fanatical:

  • Standard Edition: Includes the base game.
  • Deluxe Edition: Bundle containing the base game and the official digital soundtrack.

Pre-ordering either edition before the July 15 release grants access to the Lost Explorers' Swords Pack cosmetic DLC. This pack equips your party with custom-designed melee weapons, fitting the game's grim 17th-century expedition theme.
