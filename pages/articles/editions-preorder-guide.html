If you're trying to figure out which version of The Mound: Omen of Cthulhu to buy, here's the honest state of play: Nacon hasn't locked in official editions or pricing yet. Rather than invent numbers, this guide lays out what's actually confirmed, what's reasonable to expect, and how to make a smart buying decision before the July 15, 2026 launch.

What's actually confirmed

Two things are solid:

  • There's a physical release on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S — a boxed copy, not digital-only. PC is digital through Steam.
  • The game launches across all platforms on July 15, 2026. For the full platform rundown, see our release date & platforms breakdown.

Beyond that, official edition tiers, their contents, and prices haven't been announced. Some retailers have posted placeholder listings, but those aren't reliable — we explain why in the pricing section of our release breakdown.

Standard vs Deluxe: what to expect not yet confirmed

Nacon often ships its published games in more than one tier, and there's promotional art hinting at a premium edition — but until the storefronts go live, the table below is an expectation based on how comparable releases are usually structured, not confirmed fact:

How to pre-order on each platform

  • PC (Steam): open the official Steam page. Until a pre-purchase goes live, use Add to Wishlist to get notified.
  • PlayStation 5: search the PlayStation Store; pre-order or add to your wishlist there. A physical copy can also be reserved through retailers.
  • Xbox Series X|S: find it on the Microsoft Store to pre-order digitally, or reserve the disc version from a retailer.

Should you pre-order now?

Our honest take is the same as in our launch guide: there's little reason to pre-order with real money today. With no confirmed price and no edition details, the low-risk move is to wishlist on Steam (or follow on console) and wait. You'll get alerted the moment editions, prices, or a launch discount appear, and you can buy in then with full information. Pre-ordering is worth it only if a specific bonus or a guaranteed launch discount makes it worthwhile — and we don't know those yet.

FAQ

How many editions does The Mound have?

Not officially announced yet. A standard edition is a given, and a deluxe/premium tier is plausible based on the publisher's track record, but nothing is confirmed.

Is there a collector's or physical edition?

A standard physical disc release is confirmed for PS5 and Xbox Series X|S. No collector's edition has been announced. We'll update if one appears.

What are the pre-order bonuses?

None confirmed yet. Wishlist on Steam or follow on console to be notified the instant pre-order bonuses are revealed.
