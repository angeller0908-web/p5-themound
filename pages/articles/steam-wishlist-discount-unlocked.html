The anticipation for The Mound: Omen of Cthulhu is reaching a boiling point. Developer and publisher updates confirm that the upcoming co-op survival horror game has officially surpassed 600,000 wishlists on Steam. As part of the pre-launch milestone campaign, this achievement triggers a guaranteed 15% launch discount when the game debuts.

With the release date set for July 15, 2026, this discount offers a welcome break for players planning to dive into the abyss on day one.

Unlocking the Milestone Savings

The community has been actively pushing the game's visibility since the recent Steam Next Fest demo. By reaching the 600,000 wishlist milestone, the game unlocked its second major community tier.

Steam players will automatically see the 15% discount applied at launch. While the base price has not been finalized (estimate pending), you can check our breakdown of the expected pricing tiers and packages in our preorder and editions guide.

What This Means for Co-op Crews

For players looking to team up, this launch discount makes it easier to convince friends to join the expedition. Lower entry barriers are always great for multiplayer horror games, especially when survival depends on communication and sanity management.

If you are planning to coordinate a squad, keep in mind that the game features intense team mechanics. Check out our explanation on co-op and crossplay functionality to see how you can set up your team. Additionally, make sure your PC is ready for the dense fog and high-fidelity horrors by checking the estimated system requirements.

The Road to July 15

Passing 600,000 wishlists shows strong momentum. The developers noted that the excitement surrounding the demo was key to hitting this milestone. If you missed the demo phase, you can catch up on how the wishlist campaigns work in our wishlist and beta guide.

With less than a month remaining before the launch on July 15, 2026, the team is entering the final polish phase. Expect more details regarding server preparation and post-launch support details to emerge in the coming weeks.
