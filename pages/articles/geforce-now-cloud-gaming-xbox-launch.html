The Mound: Omen of Cthulhu Set for GeForce NOW and Xbox Launch

The Quick Answer

Release DateJuly 15, 2026
Cloud SupportNvidia GeForce NOW (Steam)
PlatformsPC (Steam), Xbox Series X|S

Low-spec PC owners and console players have reason to celebrate. With the highly anticipated release of the Lovecraftian horror title just around the corner, new store listings and cloud gaming schedules confirm that player access will be much wider than initially anticipated.

Starting July 15, 2026, players can plunge into the cursed abyss of the uncharted jungle not only via local hardware but also through cloud streaming.

GeForce NOW Support Lowers the Entry Barrier

Running a modern survival horror game on high settings usually requires premium hardware. Fortunately, community release schedules show that The Mound: Omen of Cthulhu will join the Nvidia GeForce NOW library on its launch day.

If your rig doesn't meet the game's expected system requirements, cloud streaming offers a viable alternative. This setup allows you to stream the Steam version of the game to older laptops, Chromebooks, or mobile gaming handhelds like the Logitech G Cloud, provided you have a stable internet connection.

This cloud availability is particularly useful for a game where environmental visibility and creepy atmosphere are crucial. High graphical fidelity will make spotting hidden monstrous creatures in the dark jungle canopy much easier.

Xbox Series X|S Storefront Goes Live

Console players can also start preparing their expedition gear. The official storefront page for the Xbox Series X|S version is now live on the Xbox Games Store.

The listing confirms a synchronous release with the PC edition on July 15, 2026. According to the official description, console players can expect: * Hostile Jungle Exploration: An uncharted, cursed environment where resource scarcity is a constant threat. * Stealth-Focused Survival: Success relies heavily on avoiding direct confrontation with eldritch horrors rather than fighting them head-on. * Sanity Penalties: Terrifying hallucinations and paranoia will distort reality as your explorer’s mind unravels.

Co-op Details and Pre-Launch Preparation

Although the game features deep co-op and solo play options, console players should note that crossplay between Xbox and Steam has not received a formal technical breakdown from the developers. We expect more details regarding lobby codes or unified friend lists closer to the official launch.

For those planning to purchase the game on day one, check out our breakdown of the different game editions and pre-order bonuses to choose the right version for your squad. Keep an eye on our portal as we approach the July 15 release date for live updates, launch day optimization guides, and early survival strategies.

FAQ

Can I play The Mound: Omen of Cthulhu on Nvidia GeForce NOW?

Yes. The game will be available on Nvidia GeForce NOW starting July 15, 2026, allowing you to stream the Steam version on low-spec computers or compatible handheld devices.

Is there a day-one Xbox launch for the game?

Yes. The official Xbox store page is now live, confirming that the game launches on Xbox Series X|S on July 15, 2026.

Will the game support crossplay between Steam and Xbox?

While online co-op is a core feature, crossplay functionality between PC and Xbox at launch is expected but not yet officially confirmed by the developer.
