With the highly anticipated launch of the survival horror title just hours away, developer updates and community tracking have painted a detailed picture of what players can expect when entering the subterranean depths. The community recently pushed the game past a major milestone on Steam, securing a direct financial benefit for early adopters. However, console collectors looking to secure physical copies for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X may need to adjust their calendars due to unconfirmed logistics adjustments. 

Here is a breakdown of the latest launch news, the details of the digital early-purchase rewards, the contents of the Deluxe Edition, and what the reported physical release delays mean for console players.

## The 800,000 Wishlist Milestone and Steam Launch Discount

Indie and AA developers frequently use community wishlisting goals to build momentum ahead of a launch, but the team behind this Lovecraftian expedition has tied these milestones directly to player savings. Upon passing the threshold of 800,000 wishlists on Steam, the developer confirmed that the third and final pre-launch milestone has been unlocked.

This achievement guarantees a **20% launch discount** on Steam starting tomorrow, July 15, 2026. For a game that combines squad-based coordination with tense sanity management, reducing the entry price makes it significantly easier to recruit a full team of four explorers without a heavy upfront investment. 

While the official base pricing tiers across regions are detailed in our [Release Date, Platforms, and Price](release-date-platforms-price.html) breakdown, this 20% promotional window is expected to run for the first two weeks post-launch, lowering the initial barrier to entry on PC. The discount is exclusive to Steam digital copies at launch and highlights the strong community involvement that has carried the game through its public playtests.

---

## Steam Launch Window Bonus: The Lost Explorers' Swords Pack

In addition to the launch discount, early buyers on PC will receive a specific digital reward. Steam players who purchase the game during the first two weeks after the July 15 release will receive the **Lost Explorers' Swords Pack** at no extra cost. 

Unlike traditional pre-order bonuses that disappear the moment the game launches, this early-purchase window accommodates players who prefer to wait for initial launch-day impressions and performance reviews before spending their money. The pack is expected to feature cosmetic sword skins referencing historical archaeological expeditions that went missing in the deep jungles of Oklahoma—a direct nod to the narrative setup of K'n-yan.

While primary combat in the game relies on firearms, flares, and defensive tools to ward off underground horrors, melee weapons serve as a vital last line of defense. Conservation of ammunition is key when dealing with aggressive subterranean threats, making early-access melee cosmetics a welcome addition to an explorer's inventory.

## Deluxe Edition Contents and Lovecraftian Add-Ons

For players looking at console versions or premium packages, retail listings have detailed the contents of the Deluxe Edition. Available for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X, the Deluxe Edition contains several pieces of add-on content designed to expand both character customization and narrative depth:

*   **Fortune Hunters Characters Pack:** This pack introduces additional playable character models to the roster, allowing players to diversify their team's appearance during co-op expeditions. 
*   **Temple of Yig Exclusive Mission:** This side quest introduces a unique expedition location themed around Yig, the Father of Serpents in Cthulhu Mythos lore. This mission ties back to the ancient history of K'n-yan, Yoth, and N'kai, which we explore in detail in our article on the [Lovecraft Novella Kn'yan Yoth N'kai](lovecraft-novella-knyan-yoth-nkai.html).
*   **Abyssal Gear Pack:** A collection of dark, weathered gear cosmetics that reflect the psychological toll of spending too much time in the deep underground.

These packs align with the game's focus on atmosphere and squad customization. For a complete comparison of what each tier offers, including digital and physical pre-orders, check out our [Editions and Pre-order Guide](editions-preorder-guide.html).

## Console Physical Release Delay (Expected/Not Confirmed)

While the digital launch remains firmly on track for July 15, 2026, across all platforms, community reports from retail tracking networks indicate a potential disruption for physical media collectors. Weekly physical game release schedules have flagged a potential delay for the boxed Deluxe Edition on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X.

According to these retail lists, the physical copies may have their release date pushed back to **August 6, 2026**. 

### What is the Cause of the Potential Delay?
*   **Logistical Adjustments:** Physical manufacturing and distribution pipelines often run into certification or printing delays, forcing developers to push back physical disc shipments to avoid partial stock shortages.
*   **Day-One Patch Integration:** Sometimes physical releases are slightly delayed to ensure the disc version contains a more stable build of the game, minimizing the size of the mandatory download required upon inserting the disc.
*   **Unconfirmed Status:** As of today, July 14, the developer has not issued a formal statement delaying the console versions. It is highly likely that digital store versions on the PlayStation Network and Xbox Live will still unlock on July 15, meaning only those waiting for a physical disc will need to wait until August 6.

Players who strictly buy physical games should verify their pre-order status with their local retailers to confirm if their store pickup dates have been pushed to August.
