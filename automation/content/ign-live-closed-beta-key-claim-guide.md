The wait for eldritch horror extraction is getting shorter. Ahead of its scheduled launch on July 15, 2026, developer team and IGN have teamed up to distribute Closed Beta keys for *The Mound: Omen of Cthulhu*. 

If you want to test your sanity and face subterranean monstrosities before buying, this playtest is your best opportunity. 

## How to Claim Your Closed Beta Key

The keys are being distributed through the IGN Rewards portal. To secure your access code, follow these steps:

1. Head to the official [IGN Rewards page](https://www.ign.com/rewards/ign-live-claim-your-the-mound-omen-of-cthulhu-closed-beta-key).
2. Log into your IGN account. Note that some rewards require an active IGN Plus subscription, while others are open to registered public accounts.
3. Click "Claim Key" to generate your unique product code.
4. Redeem the code on your Steam client (or the platform specified in your redemption instructions).

Keys are limited and distributed on a first-come, first-served basis. If you miss this wave, keep an eye on our [beta and demo wishlist guide](beta-demo-wishlist-guide.html) for future testing opportunities.

## What to Expect in the Closed Beta

*The Mound: Omen of Cthulhu* blends cooperative tactical shooting with classic Lovecraftian dread. Players head deep into the earth to retrieve artifacts from ancient civilizations while managing their team's mental stability.

According to early community reports from the initial closed tests, the beta focuses heavily on the squad-based extraction loop. You will be able to test the base mechanics, face a handful of the [confirmed creatures](creatures-confirmed-preview.html), and experience how the claustrophobic environments affect your character's sanity. 

Before jumping in, make sure your PC is ready by checking the [expected system requirements](system-requirements-expected.html).

## Cooperative Survival is Key

Going in alone is highly discouraged. The game is designed around a 4-player co-op loop. You will need to coordinate resources, share ammunition, and watch each other's backs when the darkness starts playing tricks on your eyes. You can read more about how matchmaking and solo play function in our [co-op and crossplay breakdown](co-op-crossplay-solo-explained.html).

If you manage to grab a key, we suggest reading our [beginners' guide](beginners-guide-10-things.html) to help you survive your very first descent into the mound.
