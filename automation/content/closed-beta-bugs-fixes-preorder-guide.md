## Critical Beta Bugs and How to Avoid Them

As an early test build, this version has some rough edges. Several known issues can crash the game or break your controls. Here are the issues developers have documented and the quickest ways to bypass them:

### Centipede Hallucination Crash
* **The Bug:** Engaging or attacking the small centipedes that spawn around the Logbook Camp during the hallucination phase triggers an immediate crash.
* **The Fix:** Simply avoid attacking these specific centipedes during the onboarding contract. Let the sequence play out.

### Guidebook UI & Camera Lockup
* **The Bug:** Exiting the guidebook menu by pressing the ESC key, or canceling the "return from boat" prompt, can permanently disconnect your mouse cursor from camera control.
* **The Fix:** Always click the on-screen button prompt to close the guidebook. Do not press ESC. If your camera controls are already locked, you will need to restart the game to restore them.

### Network Light Sync Issues
* **The Bug:** Clients sometimes fail to see the host's lantern or torch light during expeditions.
* **The Fix:** This is a minor network sync error and does not affect gameplay progression or [sanity mechanics](sanity-system-explained.html), though it does make dark tunnels much creepier.

### Inventory Slot Mismatch
* **The Bug:** The game defaults to selecting your first inventory slot upon starting an expedition, rather than preserving whatever slot you had highlighted before launching.
* **The Fix:** Just double-check your selected slot when stepping off the boat.
