## Direct Steam Purchase vs. Authorized Key Sellers

When preparing for this cooperative PVE extraction shooter, it is essential to source your game keys from licensed distributors. Fanatical is a fully authorized partner of publisher Nacon, meaning keys purchased there support the developers at ACE Team directly and are guaranteed to activate without issue on Steam.

Buying from approved key sellers often comes with minor perks, such as store-specific loyalty rewards, coupons for future purchases, or promotional bundles. However, unlike Steam's direct store page, refund policies on third-party keys can be more restrictive once a key has been revealed or activated. If you value Steam's two-hour play window refund policy, buying directly through Steam remains the safest path.
